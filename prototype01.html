Why an Adult Family Home?

The difference a real home makes

An Adult Family Home is a state-licensed private residence—not an institution—where a small group of residents receive hands-on, personalized care. It is the closest alternative to having a dedicated caregiver in your loved one’s own home.

Adult Family Home Large Facility

What We Provide

Comprehensive care under one roof

Both homes offer a full spectrum of support services, each tailored to the individual resident’s care plan and adjusted as needs evolve over time.

Personal Care

Bathing, dressing, grooming, and hygiene provided with dignity and attentiveness.

Medication Management

Accurate administration, scheduling, and coordination with prescribing physicians.

Memory Care

Structured, stimulating routines for residents living with Alzheimer’s or dementia.

Meal Preparation

Home-cooked, diet-appropriate meals prepared fresh daily in a real kitchen.

Mobility Assistance

Transfer support, fall prevention, and exercise guidance to maintain independence.

Respite Care

Short-term placement so family caregivers can rest, without disrupting routines.

Hospice Support

Compassionate end-of-life care coordination alongside your hospice provider.

Housekeeping

Laundry, cleaning, and home upkeep so every resident lives in a clean, calm environment.
